Phone Dashboard

This site hosts a Flask-based data exploration app for a smartphone pricing dataset from Kaggle. It combines preprocessing, descriptive statistics, and visual analysis to compare brands, operating systems, release trends, and value-for-money signals across the market based on the dataset.

The app loads a single CSV into pandas, applies cleaning and feature engineering, then serves charts generated with matplotlib. Most visuals are pre-rendered as SVG files for fast page loads and consistent rendering.

  • Dataset coverage: device brand, OS, screen size, battery, RAM, storage, weight, video capabilities, and price.
  • Preprocessing: resolution split into width/height, announcement year extraction, outlier handling, and known data corrections.
  • Analysis modes: distribution views, brand-level comparisons, yearly trends, and feature-correlation exploration.

Data Preprocessing

Before analysis, the following preprocessing is performed on the original data:

1. The resolution column is split into separate width and height columns.

2. A new announcement_year column is extracted from announcement_date.

3. Both resolution and announcement_date columns are removed from the dataframe.

4. Outliers in weight and price are removed, and known storage anomalies are corrected.
