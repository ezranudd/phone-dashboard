What makes a phone expensive — and which ones are worth it?

An interactive analysis of a Kaggle smartphone dataset. A price-prediction model, a value ranking, and market-tier clustering turn raw specs into two answers: what drives price, and which phones are the best value. Built with Flask, pandas, and scikit-learn; charts rendered client-side with Plotly.

Methodology & data

Data Preprocessing

Before analysis, the following preprocessing is performed on the original data:

1. The resolution column is split into separate width and height columns.

2. A new announcement_year column is extracted from announcement_date.

3. Both resolution and announcement_date columns are removed from the dataframe.

4. Outliers in weight and price are removed, and known storage anomalies are corrected.
